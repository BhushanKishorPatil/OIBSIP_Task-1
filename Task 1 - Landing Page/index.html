About Us

We are an NGO in India, which has been, since over a decades providing children without parental care or at the risk of losing it, a value chain of quality care services that goes beyond childcare alone, ensuring wholesome child development.We are an independent, non-governmental and non-profit organisation working for the holistic development of parentless children, women and children belonging to vulnerable families. Founded in 2010 in Austria, SOS Children’s Villages began its operations in India in 2012. For over 12 years, we have reached out to over 25,000 children.
